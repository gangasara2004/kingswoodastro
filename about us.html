About us

Who are we? 

Founded, in March of 1996, the Astronomical Society of Kingswood College is a leader in the space of astronomical academia at school level in Sri Lanka. With more than 300 members from grades 6 to 13 at present, we strive every day to pave the path for aspiring astronomers in the Kingswood circle. Apart from the annual astro-night we have been holding consecutively from the year 1997 to present we also organise many other events to improve public interest in astronomy and to increase the knowledge on the subject in Sri Lanka.
